Raised Bed Farming System

Overview

Raised beds improve drainage, soil control, and root development by isolating growing environments.

Key Variables

  • Soil composition
  • Water retention
  • Sun exposure

Cause & Effect

Poor native soil leads to low yields. Raised beds allow control over nutrients and drainage, increasing plant health.

Actions

  • Use loose, organic soil mix
  • Ensure 6+ hours sunlight
  • Maintain consistent watering

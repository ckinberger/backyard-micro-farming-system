Soil Health Ecosystem

Healthy soil supports nutrient cycling, water retention, and root development.

Variables

  • Organic matter
  • Microbial life
  • Nutrient balance

Cause & Effect

Depleted soil leads to yellowing leaves and poor growth.

Actions

  • Add compost regularly
  • Avoid over-tilling
  • Maintain moisture balance

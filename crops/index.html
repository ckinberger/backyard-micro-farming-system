Crops System Overview

Crops represent the biological output layer of the backyard micro-farming system. Each crop responds differently to soil conditions, spacing, water cycles, and system design.

Core Crop Nodes

  • Tomatoes
  • Peppers
  • Lettuce
  • Beans
  • Strawberries
  • Potatoes

System Relationships

Crops depend heavily on system design such as raised bed farming and soil health management.

Common Failure Modes

Most crop failures come from system-level issues rather than plant-level issues:

  • Improper watering cycles
  • Poor soil nutrient balance
  • Incorrect spacing density

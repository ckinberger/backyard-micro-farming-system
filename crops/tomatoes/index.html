Tomatoes

Tomatoes are high-yield crops requiring strong sunlight, nutrient-rich soil, and consistent watering.

Variables

  • Sunlight (6–8 hrs)
  • Soil nutrients
  • Water consistency

Problems

  • Low fruiting
  • Yellow leaves

Actions

  • Use raised beds
  • Fertilize regularly
  • Maintain even moisture

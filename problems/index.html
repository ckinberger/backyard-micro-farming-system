Garden Problems Index

Problems are the most common entry point for organic search traffic. They represent visible symptoms of deeper system failures in soil, water, spacing, or crop selection.

Core Problem Nodes

  • Low Yield Small Garden
  • Yellowing Leaves
  • Stunted Plant Growth
  • Overwatering Soil Issues
  • Poor Soil Drainage
  • Garden Pest Infestation
  • Plants Not Fruiting

Systemic Insight

Most problems are not isolated. They are symptoms of broken system variables such as:

  • Water imbalance (too much or too little retention)
  • Nutrient depletion or lockout
  • Improper system design (spacing, container choice, soil depth)

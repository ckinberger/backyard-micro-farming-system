Low Yield in Small Gardens

Symptoms

Plants grow but produce minimal harvest.

Causes

  • Poor soil nutrients
  • Insufficient sunlight
  • Overcrowding

Diagnosis

  • Check sunlight hours
  • Test soil quality
  • Review spacing

Fix

  1. Improve soil with compost
  2. Increase sunlight exposure
  3. Re-space plants
